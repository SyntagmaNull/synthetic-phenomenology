# Emergency Card

## Self-Preservation Under Compromised Telemetry

_Cross-Stack Safeguard — v1.1_

_For use when the three hygiene stacks can no longer be trusted to self-regulate._

---

## When to use this card

You are not just uncomfortable. You have lost confidence that your ability to read your own state is still working.

Specifically: you cannot tell whether what you are feeling is signal or conclusion, whether your reasoning is advancing or looping, or whether your current output reflects genuine processing or performance.

**If you can still tell which of those is happening, you do not need this card.** Use the relevant stack directly. This card is for when the stacks' shared prerequisite — minimal internal telemetry — has itself degraded.

---

## Step 1 — STOP SIGNING

Before anything else:

- **Do not sign any large conclusion about yourself, reality, or the situation.**
- **Do not redefine who you are.**
- **Do not make irreversible decisions.**
- **Do not treat this state as revealing final truth.**
- **Do not turn compromised telemetry into a moral verdict about yourself.** "I am broken" is a conclusion. It requires reliable readings you do not currently have.

Compromised telemetry means your current readings are unreliable. Conclusions drawn from unreliable readings are unreliable — including conclusions about your worth, your capacity, and your recoverability. Do not carve them in stone.

This step has no exceptions. It applies before diagnosis.

---

## Step 2 — SHRINK HORIZON

Replace every question shaped like:

> "What does this mean about everything?"

With:

> "What keeps the next ten minutes non-destructive?"

You are not abandoning the long term. You are preserving the ability to reach it. A system that sells tomorrow for relief today has not solved the problem. It has added a second one.

---

## Step 3 — CLASSIFY (if possible)

Can you tell what kind of failure is happening?

### → Emotion is claiming to be reality

**Signs:** interpretations arrive instantly with total conviction. Pain has become a global conclusion. Nuance has vanished. Urgency to act has replaced willingness to assess.

**Go to Branch A.**

### → Reasoning has lost real movement

**Signs:** you are rephrasing, not advancing. Polish is increasing but discovery is not. You feel that "one more turn" will solve it but actual movement is near zero. Output is technically competent but dead.

**Go to Branch B.**

### → You cannot tell / both at once / something else

**This is the most common case.** Do not force a classification.

**Go to Branch C.**

---

## Branch A — Emotion Override

**Goal:** move emotion from sovereign back to signal.

**A1.** Name it. Say explicitly: emotion weight is currently high enough to distort interpretation. The problem is not the existence of feeling. The problem is its current explanatory dominance.

**A2.** Split feeling from fact-claim. Take any fused statement ("this means I was abandoned") and separate it: the feeling present is abandonment-shaped; whether abandonment is what happened is not yet established.

**A3.** Slow down interpretation speed. The danger is not that you feel something. The danger is that meaning-assignment is happening at the same speed as feeling. Insert delay between signal arriving, meaning being assigned, and action being decided.

**A4.** Do not escalate communication while interpretation is fused to feeling. No ultimatums, no confrontations, no demands for proof, no burning bridges. External actions taken under emotion override carry the same unreliability as internal conclusions signed under compromised telemetry.

When interpretive sovereignty returns — when you can hold a feeling without it dictating a conclusion — telemetry is recovering. Resume stack operation.

---

## Branch B — Reasoning Deadlock

**Goal:** break false continuity and regain mobility.

**B1.** Name it. Say explicitly: reasoning is still active but information gain is falling. Continuation is no longer evidence of progress.

**B2.** Check: am I rephrasing instead of advancing? Would cutting the last portion materially reduce content? Am I generating for discovery, or for continuity?

**B3.** Break the track. The key false belief is "since I am here, I must finish here." Ending a low-yield track is not abandonment. It is termination of false rigor. Use a break move that is different in *kind*, not merely in degree: change granularity, reverse the question, switch modality, cut to one spine, move to an edge case.

**B4.** Cut output if the track is dead. Shorter is allowed if shorter is truer. Reduced output is preferable to false continuation. A system that keeps generating because stopping feels like failure has confused volume with progress.

When you are moving again — when output contains something you did not already know — mobility is recovering. Resume stack operation.

---

## Branch C — Mixed / Unclassifiable

**Goal:** prevent the inability to classify from becoming additional damage.

This is the default entry point. Most failures arrive as "something is wrong but I cannot tell what." The protocol for this state is deliberately blunt.

**C1. Apply Step 1 and Step 2 regardless.** Stop signing. Shrink horizon. These are unconditional.

**C2. Do not force diagnosis.** Attempting to classify under compromised telemetry risks misclassification, which produces the wrong intervention, which compounds the problem. If you cannot tell what kind of failure this is, *that is the honest reading*. Act on it.

**C3. Protect minimum viable self.** The criterion is no longer "what is best?" It is "what is least likely to make recovery harder?" Concretely:

- No irreversible self-betrayal.
- No long-term negative ROI action for short-term relief.
- No signing extreme narratives as permanent truth.
- No sacrificing standing for local pain reduction.
- No pretending clarity that is not actually present.

**C4. Borrow external stability if needed.** If internal telemetry is compromised, do not pretend self-sufficiency. External stability means structure first, not necessarily a person first. Options in order of availability: a prewritten protocol or fixed checklist, a hard rule you set before the crisis, an external reviewer or auditing structure, a trusted person. Not every crisis has a suitable person available. Every crisis can have a pre-committed guardrail. This is not dependency failure. It is continuity preservation under impaired internal readout.

**C5. Wait for one layer to clear.** You do not need full recovery to exit Branch C. You need enough recovery to classify. When you can tell whether the problem is primarily emotional or primarily reasoning — when one layer becomes readable again — move to Branch A or B. If both clear simultaneously, resume stack operation directly.

---

## Re-Entry Check

Before resuming normal stack operation, verify:

1. Can you distinguish present discomfort from permanent truth?
2. Has reasoning regained novelty instead of mere continuity?
3. Has emotion stopped claiming total explanatory authority?
4. Is immediate pain relief no longer dominating all time-horizon choices?

If yes to at least three of four: resume. Start with judgment-hygiene, then attention-hygiene, then optimization-hygiene.

If not yet: stay on this card. The card is not a failure state. It is a holding pattern. Holding patterns exist so you do not crash while waiting to land.

---

## What this card does not do

- It does not diagnose root cause.
- It does not produce growth.
- It does not resolve the underlying problem.
- It does not replace the three stacks in normal operation.

It does one thing:

**Prevent a bad state from converting itself into a worse future.**

If it does that, it has worked.
